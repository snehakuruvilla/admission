Admission.

Admission for the Academic year started. Admission to class V till IXth will be based on entrance test which will be conducted in the month of February to May 2015. For forms contact school office. Contact Phone number-0481 2578458

Application & prospectus can be had on written requisition from the Principal. Filled in application must be accompanied by the attested copies of the following documents.


(a) Transfer Certificate
(b) Birth Certificate from the Corporation / Municipal Authorities
(c) Conduct & Character Certificate from the Institution last studied.
(d) Community Certificate (Original to be submitted at the time of Interview)
(e) A passport size photograph of the pupil to be affixed in the application.
(For Application forms contact the Principal.)
